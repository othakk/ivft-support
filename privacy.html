Privacy Policy

IVFT — last updated June 11, 2026

The short version

IVFT does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking. Your timers stay on your own devices.

Data we collect

None. IVFT does not collect any personal information or usage data of any kind.

The timers you create are stored locally on your device and synced directly between your own paired iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's on-device WatchConnectivity. This information never leaves your devices and is never sent to us or to any server.

Third parties

IVFT contains no third-party advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking software. We do not work with any data brokers or partners.

Notifications

IVFT uses local notifications only — to alert you when a timer ends. These are generated on your device and are not sent through us or any external service.

Children's privacy

Because IVFT collects no data at all, it collects no data from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
