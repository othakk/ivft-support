Simple, reliable countdown timers for iPhone and Apple Watch.

What it does

Create named countdown timers, run several at once, and get alerted the moment each one finishes. Timers stay in sync automatically across your iPhone and Apple Watch, and the Apple Watch widget keeps your soonest timer one glance away.

Common questions

  • Do my timers keep running if I lower my wrist or close the app? Yes — each timer tracks its exact end time, so the countdown stays accurate.
  • Can I turn off the sound or haptic? Yes — each alert has its own on/off switch in Settings.
  • Do my timers sync between my iPhone and Apple Watch? Yes — start or edit a timer on one device and it updates on the other automatically.
  • Does the app collect any of my data? No. IVFT has no account and no sign-up. Your timers stay on your device and are never sent to a server.
