BagWatch

IV bag infusion timers for nurses — right on your wrist.

What it does

Start an IV infusion timer in two taps, run a separate countdown for every patient, and label each one with a quick room/bed/fluid chip. BagWatch alerts you with a haptic, sound, and a red “DONE” so you can stay ahead of the pump alarm.

Common questions

  • Do my timers keep running if I lower my wrist or the app closes? Yes — each timer tracks its exact end time, so the countdown stays accurate.
  • Can I turn off the sound or haptic? Yes — each alert (haptic, sound, visual) has its own on/off switch in Settings.
  • Does it store patient information? No. BagWatch stores no patient data and asks you to use room/bed/fluid labels only.
Important: BagWatch is a convenience reminder aid only. It does not monitor or control the infusion and is not a substitute for the infusion pump’s own alarms. Always rely on the pump and your clinical judgment. Use room, bed, or fluid labels — never patient names.
